From Concept to Completion your Interior Designer Works in Kerala

At Coax Architecture Studio, we follow a comprehensive approach to bring your vision to life. Whether it's home interior design or office interior design, our process includes concept development, planning, material selection, and flawless execution. As top interior designers in Kerala, we ensure seamless results every step of the way.

01. initial consultation

We start with a one-on meeting to understand your vision preferences and requirement.

02. design planning

This involves selecting materials, and layouts, furnishings, as well as creating 3D renderings.

03. project execution

With the design plans in this place, we manage and coordinate all aspects of the projects.

04. final review

After completing project we conduct a thorough walkthrough with you to review the space.
